Dish Name: Assidat Zgougou

Origin: [Tunisia]

Ingredients:

  • Zgougou (Aleppo pine seeds)
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Garnishes: almonds, nuts, pistachios, decorative cream

Preparation:Prepare the zgougou base: Wash and grind zgougou seeds. Mix with water, filter to extract the milk. Add flour and sugar to the liquid. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Make the cream topping: Mix milk, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook while stirring until it becomes a smooth custard. Assemble the dessert: Pour zgougou base into cups or bowls. Add a layer of custard on top. Decorate: Garnish with nuts (almonds, pistachios, hazelnuts) and cream patterns. Chill and serve cold.
